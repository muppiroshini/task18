MS Dhoni
Episode 1

The movie follows the journey of Mahendra Singh Dhoni, a budding cricketer from Ranchi becoming the most successful captain of Indian Cricket Team . The movie begins its screenplay with the ICC Cricket World Cup Final (India vs Sri Lanka) in the Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai where Mahendra Singh Dhoni (Sushant Singh Rajput) is watching the match live on screen. He decides to take the stand, and tells the authorities that he would go for batting if the wicket falls down. Dhoni takes up his bat and leads down the stairs to the roar of the crowd. The movie then sets to a flashback in Ranchi, Bihar, India set in 7 July 1981, the day on which Dhoni was born. Pan Singh (Anupam Kher) is Dhoni's father & Jayanti Gupta (Bhumika Chawla) is his sister. As a young boy Dhoni is the goalkeeper for his school team. Baneerji (Rajesh Sharma) is Dhoni's school coach and watches him in goalkeeping practice. He needs a wicket-keeper for his school cricket team. Pan Singh is the water manager for the residential colony of Meco limited, a PSU. As such, he is also in charge of watering the local cricket stadium. He wants Dhoni to study and become something in life. But he doesn't stop MS to pick up sports. Banerjee convinces Dhoni to pick up cricket and asks him to concentrate on wicket-keeping (Dhoni wanted to be a batsman). Still, Dhoni steals every available moment from his coach's eyes to practice batting. Soon, he is winning matches for his school, in crunch situations, with his batting. soon, Dhoni asks Banerjee to let him open batting for his team, which Banerjee reluctantly allows. DHoni demonstrates his ability to pull crowds to grounds with his electric batting.
